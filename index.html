About Nominalysis

Nominalysis is a specialized thermal engineering consultancy dedicated to supporting satellite and payload teams. Our mission is to ensure spacecraft operate reliably in harsh orbital environments through precise thermal analysis, simulation, and testing.

We bridge the gap between design intent and operational performance, helping clients avoid costly thermal failures and maximize system efficiency.
